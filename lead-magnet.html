The Missed Call Interceptor

Recovers lost leads within 90 seconds — before they call your competitor.

Every missed call is a lost sale in progress. A potential customer called you — they were ready to book, buy, or inquire. Then life happened: your team was busy, it was after hours, or the volume was too high. Within 5 minutes of that missed call, 78% of those leads have moved on to try someone else.

The Missed Call Interceptor solves this by triggering an automatic, personalized SMS response within 90 seconds of every missed call. No humans required. No delay. Just instant recovery.

Trigger
Missed inbound call detected
Response Time
Under 90 seconds
Stack Required
AI agent + phone number (existing or new)
Time to Deploy
1–2 days
Sample Recovery SMS — Copy & Customize
Hi [First Name]! This is [Business Name] — so sorry we missed your call just now. We'd love to help you out. What were you looking for today? I can check availability right now and get you sorted. Just reply here and we'll take it from there! 😊 — [Your Name], [Business Name]
📈 Expected Result: Recover 15–25% of previously lost leads in the first 30 days. Average reply rate on this message template: 32–38% in service businesses.

How to Deploy It

  1. Set up your AI agent with access to your phone/communication system
  2. Configure the missed call trigger — most phone systems support webhooks or forwarding rules
  3. Write your recovery message using the template above, personalized to your business tone
  4. Set a follow-up sequence — if they don't reply in 2 hours, send a second message offering your booking link
  5. Test with 3 intentional missed calls to verify timing and message delivery

The Lead Gen Machine

Outreach that never sleeps — finding, contacting, and following up with prospects 24/7.

Most business owners do outreach in bursts — a week of hustle, then nothing for a month. Revenue follows the same pattern: spiky and unpredictable. The Lead Gen Machine creates a consistent, repeatable pipeline that runs regardless of your schedule, your mood, or what else is on your plate.

This agent handles three stages autonomously: finding qualified prospects, sending a personalized first contact, and executing a 5-touch follow-up sequence if they don't respond. Most leads convert between touch 3 and touch 5 — which means most businesses are leaving money on the table by giving up after one message.

Outreach Channels
Email + SMS (dual-channel converts 40% better)
Follow-Up Sequence
5 touches over 14 days — automated

Typically 3–7 days from deployment

Input Required
1 hour/month — brand voice, topics, key offers
Output
30 days of posts across your active platforms
Content Formats
Short posts, threads, carousels, hooks, CTAs
Monetization Path
Inbound leads, product promotion, authority building

The Client Reactivator

Mine your existing database for revenue hiding in plain sight.

Your most valuable leads aren't strangers — they're people who already said yes to you once. Past clients who went quiet. Leads who inquired but never booked. Contacts who bought once and haven't returned. This is money sitting in your database right now, and most businesses never go back for it.

The Client Reactivator runs a warm, personalized outreach campaign to contacts who haven't engaged in 60–90 days. No hard sell — just a well-timed, relevant message that reminds them you exist and makes it easy to take the next step.

Target Contacts
Dormant for 60–90 days (past clients + old leads)
Message Tone
Warm, personal, low-pressure — never spammy
Cadence
3-message sequence over 10 days
Average Conversion
8–15% of dormant contacts respond
Reactivation Message 1 — Day 1
Hi [First Name]! It's been a little while since we connected and I've been thinking about you. We just opened up some availability and wanted to reach out to our favorite past clients first before posting publicly. Would you like to get back on the schedule? I'd love to take care of you again. — [Your Name], [Business Name] [Booking Link]
💰 Expected Result: A list of 200 dormant contacts typically yields 16–30 reactivated clients. At an average transaction value of $200, that's $3,200–$6,000 from a list you already had — with zero ad spend.

How to Deploy It

  1. Export your contact list from your CRM or booking system
  2. Filter for dormant contacts — anyone who hasn't booked or purchased in 60–90 days
  3. Segment by client type — past clients get a warmer message than cold leads
  4. Deploy the 3-message sequence — Day 1 (warm reconnect), Day 4 (value add or offer), Day 10 (final soft close)
  5. Remove responders from the sequence and route them into your active pipeline

The Review Builder

One more star on Google is worth more than any ad you'll ever run.

Harvard Business School research found that a 1-star increase in a business's Yelp rating leads to a 5–9% revenue increase. Google reviews have an equivalent — often stronger — effect on local businesses. Yet most businesses never systematically ask for reviews. They assume happy clients will leave them. They rarely do — unless they're prompted at the right moment.

The Review Builder identifies the perfect post-transaction window (typically 18–24 hours after a completed service) and sends a warm, personal review request with a direct link. Frictionless. Timely. Effective.

Trigger
18–24 hours after completed appointment or purchase
Platform Target
Google (primary), Yelp, Facebook (secondary)
Review Rate
Average 28–35% of asked clients leave a review
Revenue Impact
+1 star Google rating = 5–9% revenue increase
Review Request SMS — 24 Hours Post-Service
Hi [First Name]! We hope you're absolutely loving your results from yesterday 🌟 Your experience means the world to us — and so does your feedback. If you have 60 seconds, would you mind sharing a quick Google review? It makes a huge difference for our small business. [Direct Google Review Link] Thank you so much. We can't wait to see you again! 💛 — [Your Name], [Business Name]
⭐ Expected Result: Businesses running this agent consistently collect 8–12 new reviews per month per 30 clients served. Within 90 days, most see a measurable increase in inbound organic discovery from Google Maps.

How to Deploy It

  1. Get your Google review link — search your business on Google → "Get more reviews" → copy the direct link
  2. Connect your appointment or purchase system to trigger the message 18–24 hours after completion
  3. Personalize the message with the client's name and the specific service they received
  4. Set a single follow-up — if they don't review within 5 days, send one gentle reminder
  5. Monitor and respond to every review that comes in — Google rewards active responders
